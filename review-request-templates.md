## üîó **HOW TO GET YOUR GOOGLE REVIEW LINK**

1. Go to your Google Business Profile
2. Click on "Home"
3. Find "Get more reviews" section
4. Click "Share review form"
5. Copy the link (looks like: g.page/r/XXXXX/review)
6. Use a URL shortener (bit.ly) to create short link for SMS
7. Create QR code at qr-code-generator.com

**Your Direct Review Link:**
```
https://g.page/r/[YOUR-BUSINESS-ID]/review
```

## üí° **PRO TIPS**

1. **Make it EASY** - QR codes are crucial (people won't type URLs)
2. **Ask at the RIGHT TIME** - when customers are happiest
3. **Incentivize** - Rewards points work great
4. **Be SPECIFIC** - "If you loved our honey pizza, please review!"
5. **Follow Up QUICKLY** - Ask within 24 hours while memory is fresh
6. **Thank Reviewers** - Respond to every review personally
7. **Never Buy Reviews** - Google will penalize you
8. **Be Patient** - Building reviews takes time but compounds
